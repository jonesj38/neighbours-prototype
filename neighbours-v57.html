Safety

Help & Safety

Tips and guidelines to stay safe when posting or working jobs on Neighbours.

If you're posting a job
Poster Safety Tips

Before the job:

• Be clear about the job scope and expectations

• Use in-app messaging until you're comfortable

• Don't share unnecessary personal information

During the job:

• If possible, have another adult present

• Put valuables away before someone comes over

• Don't let someone do unsafe or unlicensed work

Trust your instincts:

• Cancel if something feels uncomfortable

• Report inappropriate behaviour immediately

If you're working a job
Worker Safety Tips

Before arriving:

• Confirm the job details before you go

• Tell someone where you're going

• Bring appropriate tools and protection

At the job:

• Don't enter a home if you feel unsafe

• Don't do work outside your ability

• Don't do electrical, plumbing, or gas work unless licensed

Trust your instincts:

• Leave and report if the situation feels wrong

• Don't accept suspicious payment arrangements

Need help right now?

If you feel unsafe, leave the situation and call 911.

To report a problem with a user or job, use the Report a Problem feature in the menu.

Refund Policy

Information about posting fees, payments, and refunds on Neighbours.

Posting Fee

Your first 3 job posts are free. After that, each post costs $1.99.

Posting fees are generally non-refundable once a job is published, except where Neighbours determines there was a technical error, duplicate charge, fraud, or other exceptional circumstance.

Job Payment

Neighbours currently charges only the posting fee. Workers and posters arrange job payment directly between themselves.

Neighbours does not process, hold, or guarantee job payments at this time.

Disputes

If you have a payment issue or disagreement about a job:

1. Try to resolve it directly with the other person

2. If that doesn't work, report the problem through the app

3. Neighbours may review reports and take account action

Note: Neighbours is not responsible for guaranteeing work quality, payment between users, property damage, injuries, or user conduct.

Request a Refund

If you believe you're eligible for a posting fee refund, contact us through the Help & Feedback form.
